    Deliver Your Theme

    task
    You use the Cloud Admin to make your theme available to sites. You can make it available to all sites by uploading it to the Theme Gallery, or you can deliver it to a single site.

    Deliver a Cloud​ Theme to All Customers

    ​You use the Cloud Admin to make your theme available from a tab in the Theme Gallery.

    ​ To make your theme available from the gallery:

    1. In the Cloud Admin, click the Themes tab. Any current custom themes are displayed on the page.

    2. Click Create New Theme.

    3. Enter a theme name. This is what will be displayed to your customers.

    4. Click Upload Theme Zip. Navigate to the directory where you exported your theme and upload your theme’s zip.

    5. Click Create Theme. Your new theme displays in the Cloud Admin using the screenshot you created.

    ​Once added, your theme displays on your company’s theme tab in the Theme Gallery.

    Deliver Your Theme to One Customer

    Instead of making your theme available in the gallery, you can upload a theme directly to a single site.

    To provide a theme to a single site:

    1. Export the theme to a zip file.

    2. Open the site that will use the theme in the editor and import the theme.
